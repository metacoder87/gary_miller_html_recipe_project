Cow Dabs

Description Ingredients Directions Other Versions

Description

A delictious Chocolate Peanut-Butter no-bake Oatmeal cookie. Just like Grandma used to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Sugar
  • & 3 tbsp of Cocoa
  • 1/2 Cup of Butter
  • 1/2 Cup of Condensed Milk
  • 1 cup of Peanut Butter
  • 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 3 Cups of fine Oats

Directions

  1. Sift together until finely mixed:
  2. Sugar & Cocoa
  3. Heat on Medium in 4qt pot
  4. Stir continuosly as you mix in:
  5. Butter stir until melted
  6. Condensed Milk
  7. Once Chocolate looks fine add:
  8. Peanut Butter
  9. Vanilla Extract
  10. then the Oats
  11. Shovel out with a Spoon as soon as mixed.
  12. Let Cool...if you have the Will-power.
